Kintsugi

How to play ▼

Tap or click a hexagon to spin its three wedges. On a touch screen, hold to preview the tile, then lift to spin. Where tiles meet, the wedges join into new hexagons.

When a hexagon's colors all match, its cracks are mended with gold - the art of kintsugi. Mend every hexagon to restore the piece.

Mode

Colors glazes each wedge porcelain, slate, or clay - line up all six around a junction to mend that hexagon. Slate (Hard) hides the colors: every tile is the same slate, so the spreading gold seams are your only guide. Right-click (desktop) spins a tile the other way.

Size

Small is a 19-tile board to learn on, Large grows it to 37 tiles, and Agony is a 61-tile marathon. Every puzzle is guaranteed solvable.

Break

Press Break for a fresh piece to mend. Changing Mode or Size also breaks a new piece.
